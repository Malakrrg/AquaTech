The problem: unsafe drinking water

Over 2 billion people lack access to safely managed drinking water. Existing solutions are expensive, energy‑hungry, or hard to maintain.

Contaminated sources

Rural communities often rely on surface water contaminated with bacteria, microplastics, and chemicals. Boiling is fuel‑intensive and doesn't remove heavy metals.

No reliable power

Electric filters fail during blackouts. Diesel generators are costly and dirty. There's a need for a truly off‑grid solution.

Our solution: aquaTech

A solar‑powered, self‑contained water filtration unit with real‑time quality feedback.

Energy independent

Integrated 1W solar panel + 3000mAh 18650 battery. Runs for months even without sun. Low‑power electronics (deep sleep ~50µA).

Active sterilization

Triple filtration (gravel, sand, activated carbon) plus 275nm UVC LED that neutralizes 99.9% of pathogens in 94 seconds.

Smart monitoring

pH and TDS sensors send data to an OLED display showing simple "GOOD / CHECK / DANGER" — no technical knowledge needed.

Modular & maintainable

Cartridges can be cleaned or replaced locally. Designed with rural repair in mind.

How it works
